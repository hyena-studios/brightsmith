## Decisions Made
1. CDEs are boolean flags + rationale on physical data elements (columns), not on abstract business terms — aligns with BCBS 239 and DAMA-DMBOK
2. Same business term can be CDE in one table and not another — criticality is contextual
3. PII follows identical pattern to CDE — flag on physical element, not term
4. CDE catalog (`governance/cde-catalog.json`) eliminated — "CDE list" becomes a query across contracts
5. No backward propagation — marking a gold column as CDE doesn't affect bronze/silver
6. CDEs exist wherever business terms are applied (consequence, not rule) — avoids inventing an arbitrary layer restriction
7. Business glossary drops from ~14 to 11 required fields — pure semantic definitions
